# Profitability — High-Willingness Paid Content Directions

## Top paid directions
1. AI Product Architecture Teardowns — highest differentiation and buyer urgency.
2. Research-to-Production Translation — strong willingness where teams need to convert papers into production decisions.
3. Production AI Engineering / Evals / Debugging — painful enterprise engineering problem, good paid appendix potential.

## Price hypothesis
- Free tier: weekly short memo + selected teardown.
- Paid individual: $9-15/month or $90-150/year.
- Team/pro tier: $29-49/month for templates, scorecards, code examples, office-hour style Q&A.
- Consulting upsell: $499-2,000 teardown/review package after paid audience proof.

## Unit economics
Substack takes ~10% plus Stripe processing. At $15/month, net before labor is roughly $12-13/subscriber/month. A 100 paid subscriber base can support continued validation; 500+ supports meaningful side-income if production is templated.

## 4-8 week validation metrics
- 4 public posts, 2 paid deep dives, 1 downloadable template.
- 300+ targeted visits, 100+ email subscribers, 10+ paid trial/annual subscribers or 3+ qualified consulting leads.
- Pivot if free posts get clicks but no paid conversion; No-Go if senior-builder audiences do not engage after targeted distribution.
